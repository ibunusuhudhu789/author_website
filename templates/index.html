The Author

Ibunu Suhudhu

Ibunu Suhudhu is a writer drawn to stories filled with longing, silence, emotions, and beautifully complicated people. His work often explores the fragile spaces between love, loneliness, comfort, and chaos.

Through fiction, blog posts, and personal reflections, he creates worlds where emotions are allowed to exist honestly — raw, messy, intimate, and human.
